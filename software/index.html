Fire Manifesting App

The Fire Manifesting App is a fast, flexible tool for wildland firefighting crews to generate accurate helicopter load manifests. It was originally built for Alaska fire crews due to the remote nature of Alaska fire, but it supports any fire aviation personnel needing quick, reliable load planning. To reduce the time and errors of manual manifesting, the app automates weight calculations and load configurations. Users can enter crew, tool, and equipment weights, and apply preferences like including water on every load or distributing leadership evenly. By combining automation with crew-specific control, the app minimizes stress, saves time, and enables faster response to last-minute operational changes.
